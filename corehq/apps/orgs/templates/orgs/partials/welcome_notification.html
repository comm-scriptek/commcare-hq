Projects Info

Add projects that are part of your organization. You can add a project if you are an admin member of that project. Project admins can also request to join your organization.

Teams Info

Organize your members into different teams. You can grant each team different permissions for each project. In the future, this will control which project’s data team members see in global reports and advanced analytics.

Members Info

Add members to your organization. In the future, members will be able to take advantage of organization-level features like global reports and advanced analytics.
